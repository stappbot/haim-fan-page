Biography

HAIM is a American pop band band from Los Angeles (or "lozenge-les" as I like to pronounce it).
They are three super amazing sisters who grew up with very musical parents.
Moti and Donna (their parents) taught them music from an early age with their cover band
"Rockinhaim." How cool is that!

Band members

Este Haim - bass guitar and vocals
Danielle Haim - vocals and guitar
Alana Haim - guitars, keyboard, and vocals
